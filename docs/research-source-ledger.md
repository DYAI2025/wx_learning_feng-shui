# Research and source ledger — BZG-32

## Editorial target

Build a source-oriented English guide at `https://sizhuatelier.shop/learn/wu-xing/feng-shui/` covering directions, colours, seasons, rooms, traditional applications, schools, variants and historical context without duplicating the Wu Xing cornerstone or deriving scientific efficacy from traditional models.

## Source hierarchy

1. Early textual sources for correspondences.
2. Academic reference works for historical development and school taxonomy.
3. Official web standards for SEO and accessibility.
4. Practitioner sources only when explicitly identified as lineage-specific.
5. Marketplace and unattributed web content are not truth sources.

## Chinese text policy

- `CN_SIMPLIFIED` with shared core characters only: `五行`, `木`, `火`, `土`, `金`, `水`, `生`, `克`.
- Tone-marked Pinyin: `wǔxíng`, `mù`, `huǒ`, `tǔ`, `jīn`, `shuǐ`, `shēng`, `kè`.
- No AI-generated Hanzi images or pseudo-Chinese typography.

## Method boundaries

- Form-focused and compass-focused approaches are broad categories, not rigid universal schools.
- Three Harmonies, Three Cycles, Flying Stars and Eight Mansions are named only as later lineages or methods.
- No star combinations, auspiciousness ratings, cures or personal predictions are issued.
- The five-direction matrix is not expanded into eight sectors without naming the additional system.
- Colour families are historical correspondences, not scientifically active doses.
